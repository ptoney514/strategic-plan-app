Common Procurement Inquiries

Everything you need to know about bringing StrataDash to your district's leadership team.

How does the annual billing cycle work?

We operate on a standard July 1 – June 30 fiscal year billing cycle for most districts, but we can pro-rate your first year to match your specific budget calendar. Invoices are payable via check, ACH, or Purchase Order.

Can we import our existing 5-year plan?

Yes. Our onboarding success team provides a templated CSV for bulk uploads, or we can provide a white-glove setup service for District and Enterprise tiers to manually map your existing PDF into our hierarchy.

Is there a limit on public page views?

No. We believe transparency should never be penalized. Whether you have 100 parents or 100,000 visiting your strategic dashboard, there are never extra data or bandwidth charges.

What about student data privacy?

StrataDash is designed for high-level strategic tracking. We do not require, nor do we recommend, the upload of PII (Personally Identifiable Information). We track aggregate percentages and district-wide metrics.
