Introduction

At StrataDash, we believe that strategic planning should be supported by clear data ethics. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our platform, services, and website.

By using the StrataDash services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We prioritize the security of institutional data above all else.

Information Collected

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

Institutional Data

District names, school IDs, strategic goals, and performance metrics uploaded by authorized administrative personnel.

Account Information

Professional contact details including names, email addresses, and titles used to manage access and collaboration.

How We Use Info

  • check_circle To facilitate strategic visualization and data-driven decision making.

  • check_circle To provide technical support and personalized account management.

Cookies

StrataDash uses essential cookies to maintain secure sessions. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies within the authenticated dashboard environment. Standard analytical cookies may be used on our marketing pages to improve site performance.

Student & District Data

We take student data privacy seriously. StrataDash is compliant with FERPA and COPPA guidelines.

Security

We employ industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. Access to institutional data is restricted to StrataDash employees who require it for maintenance, and all such access is audited.
